How to win or lose
  • If all the players guess out the word, the people who draw doesn’t get a point.
  • If there’s only one player who can’t figure out what the word is, the drawer will get the highest point in that turn.
  • The person who guess out the word first gets the most point.
  • The highest point that the players can get is determined by the numbers of players, e.g. if there are 8 players, the highest point the players can get will be 8.
  • The people who get the highest point wins and lowest point lose.
